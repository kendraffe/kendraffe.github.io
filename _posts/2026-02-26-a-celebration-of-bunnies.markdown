I’ve been meaning to write a real update for a while, so here’s a quick(ish) roundup of what I’ve been up to since 2019—new work on the page, plus a couple of delightful moments where my writing escaped the page entirely.

### On the page (2019–2021)

- **“No Matter”** (Lightspeed, Aug 2019): a story about time and choice and the ways we keep trying, even when we already know the ending.  
  Read it: <https://www.lightspeedmagazine.com/fiction/no-matter/>

- **“Supergiant”** (Paper Darts, Nov 2019): family history, strange relics, and the gravitational pull of what we inherit.  
  Read it: <https://www.paperdarts.org/literary-magazine/2019/11/3/supergiant>

- **“Waking Up to the Perfect Body”** (Gay Mag, Nov 2019): a personal essay about bodies, America, and the stories we’re taught to tell ourselves.  
  Read it: <https://gay.medium.com/waking-up-to-the-perfect-body-507bad2ad6a>

- **“Love Is Feudal”** (HAD, Sep 2021): yes, it’s called that. (Sometimes a title is a dare.)  
  Read it: <https://www.havehashad.com/hadposts/i-lost-a-bet-and-now-this-story-is-called-love-is-feudal>

### Off the page (2023–2025)

- **Selected Shorts at Symphony Space** (Jan 25, 2023): I got to be part of a night curated by Brooke Gladstone, alongside Mary Gordon, Meron Hadero, and David Michael Kaplan.  
  Event page: <https://www.symphonyspace.org/events/selected-shorts-the-stories-we-tell-ourselves-with-brooke-gladstone>

- **Selected Shorts podcast** (July 2025): my One Story piece **“Things I Know To Be True”** was read by Calvin Leon Smith.  
  Listen: <https://soundcloud.com/selectedshorts/ss-2024-25-46_pcastfinal>
